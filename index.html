Basic background

German is One of the major languages of the world. It is the first language of about 95 million people worldwide and the most widely spoken native language in the European Union

Varieties in German

In German linguistics, German dialects are distinguished from varieties of standard German. The varieties of standard German refer to the different local varieties of the pluricentric standard German.
